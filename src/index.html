Criminal Defence

Services

Forte Defence is led by Marco Forte, a seasoned criminal defence lawyer who is there for you when your back is against the ropes. We provide unpretentious legal services for those looking for an attentive and personalized approach to their criminal cases. What sets us apart from the fold is not only our patience, our expertise, and our impeccable knowledge of the law, but more simply, it is our recognition that our clients deserve dignity.

We are a progressive criminal defence firm that is well attuned to the various disadvantages that plague individuals caught up in the criminal justice system. While it is true that the law treats all accused as innocent until proven guilty, it’s not surprising for those charged with offences to face unfortunate stigmas. We are aware of these challenges in the law, and it is why we are fearless in our use of the Constitution as a shield to protect you from injustice.

The strength of Mr. Forte’s team is not limited to our commanding presence in the courtroom, but is also demonstrated by the compelling advocacy we engage in beyond the trial stand. This includes negotiating with police and Crown counsel alike to attain unparalleled outcomes for you. Our objective is to help you regain your footing so you can eventually move past this difficult period of your life.

Though you may have been accused of committing a criminal offence, we at Forte Defence understand how the story is never black and white. Getting arrested and charged with a crime can be a confusing and harrowing experience. An individual alone is often no match against the vast resources of the police and the state. It is therefore important that you have competent criminal defence counsel on your side in order to protect your legal rights to their fullest extent. It is also crucial that we preserve your opportunity to tell an impartial court your side of the story.

Call or meet with us today to discuss your or a loved one’s case. A consultation is always free, and we always strive to provide you with the ability to make an informed choice about who is the right lawyer for you. Whether you are calling us from the anxiety-inducing depths of the police station because you need us to arrange your bail, or you’re meeting us in person in the comforts of our office in Little Italy – you know that you can depend on us.
