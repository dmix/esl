About EIE

Education in English (EIE) is an English program based in China that aims to provide Chinese students with the English speaking, writing, and comprehension skills they need in an increasingly globalized world. The program was first established in 1988 in Toronto, Canada, at a time when China was just beginning to open its doors to the West after decades of seclusion during the Cultural Revolution.
