Benefits

for EIE teachers

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Education in English offers a competitive salary for all our teachers, as well as a great benefits package.

You will receive a monthly salary, an international airfare allowance, free pick-up from the airport, free comfortable and modern accommodations with complimentary WIFI so you can stay in touch with those back home, English-speaking support staff, and free beginner level Chinese classes to help ease your integration into Chinese society. You can set your mind at ease as you will be provided with a wealth of resources during your stay to assist you with transitioning to life in China.

In terms of employment advancement, teachers wishing to continue with EIE who have demonstrated an ability to provide above standard educational experiences for their students will be considered for a Director of Teaching Affairs (DTA) position at one of our campuses. DTAs will receive an increased salary, and more managerial responsibilities.
